Usually zoom workplace app is used for online classes, office meetings, interviews, webinars and presentations. It works on both android and apple phones and also on computers. Guide to Install Zoom Workplace App

You can easily use zoom workplace app on your windows 11 pc. Install zoom workplace app from Microsoft store or from their official website. The following are the step to install Zoom workplace app on your windows 11 pc from Microsoft store. Select Microsoft store from the start menu.

Find the Zoom Workplace app in Microsoft store

You can easily find the Zoom Workplace app in the Microsoft Store. Simply search for Zoom Workplace app and click the install button to install it. Refer to the screenshot shown below for guidance on how to install zoom workplace app on your windows 11 pc.

Zoom Workplace App Download Status

In the below screenshot that the zoom workplace app is started downloading. Here you can see 37.57 MB of 139.6 MB downloaded. You can also share or copy the link by clicking the share button near downloading. A small description provided above download status about this application.

As shown in the below screenshot that the zoom workplace app is now installed in your windows 11 pc, and the app provide a short note about zoom workplace app. It will help us to connect, communicate, and express our ideas. The app is trusted by millions of enterprises, small businesses, and individuals. You can open the app from the start menu.

Sign in to Zoom Workplace

You can sign in to the Zoom Workplace app on your windows 11 pc. After opening the application, the Zoom Workplace home screen will appear as shown in the screen short below. It comes with options such as sign in, join meeting, and sign up. click the sign in button to access your account and start using the app.

Sign in using Email or Other Accounts

You can easily Sign in to the application using email or phone number. Also Sign in through SSO, Google, Apple, Facebook or Microsoft. If you don’t have any account, you can create by clicking Sign up option and click the chat with us option for help. Here the process is continued with an email account for Sign in and click the next button to continue.

Entering Password to Sign in to Zoom Workplace

Now enter the password of your Zoom account in the password column. If you have forgotten your password, reset it by clicking “forgot password” option, you can also choose the try another way to sign in option for different sign in method. Here a password is used to sign in and click the sign in button to continue.

Passkey Setup for Zoom Sign in

You can create a passkey to sign in to your Zoom account by simply clicking next button. This passkey makes you to sign in faster in the future without entering a password. If you do not want to set a passkey, you can click the “skip for now and sign in” to continue.

Home Screen of Zoom Workplace

The home screen of Zoom Workplace is given below in the screenshot. Here you can see the overview of your meeting schedule for the day and shortcuts to start new meeting, join meeting, schedule a meeting or share your screen. You can create and manage personal notes in my notes. Zoom app provide AI companion as your personal assistant.

New Meeting Settings

You can see these options by simply clicking the new meeting button. If the “start with video” option is checked, your webcam turns on automatically when the meeting started. When you use the “Use my personal meeting ID (PMI)” option, you can use your permanent id for a meeting. And you see an ID below. This is your personal meeting ID (PMI). Secondary menu shows:

  • Copy ID: You can copy the ID to your clipboard.
  • Copy invitation: Share the full meeting details to others.
  • PMI settings: Permanent room settings.

How to Join a Meeting

You can join a meeting by simply clicking the join button. Enter the meeting ID or personal link name to join a meeting. your name will come automatically, also decide the audio and video to be off. Click join to join in. Or click on cancel to cancel the meeting.

How to Share your Screen

You can share your screen by simply clicking the share screen button. Enter the room code or meeting ID and click the share screen button to share with the meeting. click on cancel button to cancel it.

Zoom Workplace AI Companion

The AI Companion is your personal assistant this is an advanced feature in Zoom Workplace app. It helps you to search for email, chats, meetings. You can summarize or answer questions about meetings, preparing for upcoming meetings, getting someone’s profile details, find other people like team members, also you can ask AI Companion to schedule a meeting, coordinate meetings with external contacts.

My Notes

You can take notes during meetings. Open “my notes” during a meeting, click on “Type here if desired” and start typing your points, ideas, or important notes. Zoom will automatically capture transcripts, convert speech into text. You can see it under “transcript”. Click on play button to replay recorded part. Click done to save the notes automatically in your zoom account. You can upgrade for unlimited experiences.

How to Schedule a Meeting

You can schedule a meeting by simply clicking the schedule button from home page. You can give a title to your meeting, so participants can see it when they join the call. You can set the date and time to start and end the meeting. You can set the time zone for your meeting this ensures the meeting appears at the correct time for people in different region. Set your meeting repeated for daily, weekly or never. You can type names or email address in “add invitees” to add others.   

  • Join the meet using automatically generated ID/using personal meeting ID.
  • Create meeting agenda by clicking create agenda.
  • You can upload files and documents through attachments.
  • Entering with passcode/waiting room.

Advanced Settings to Schedule a Meeting

You can enable enhanced encryption or end-to-end encryption option, enable continuous meeting chat. Decide video to be on or off by host or participants. Enable computer audio use option. Calendar or google calendars can use. You can enable advanced features shown in the screenshot below.  Now you can save and schedule a meeting by simply clicking save button or you can cancel by clicking cancel button.

Instant Meeting using Meet Now Feature

You can start a meeting instantly by simply clicking the blue plus button. There are two options, to create an event and a meet now option. Schedule a meet by simply clicking create event option. Click on Meet now to start an instant meeting. Your personal meeting room window will appear as in the screenshot. Click the start button to start your meeting this is useful for urgent discussions or quick meetings.  

Audio Settings

You can control your microphone and speaker during a meeting by simply clicking the audio button. Click the drop up arrow to explore more features. Select, test and adjust your audio devices to hear others clearly.

Video Settings

Control your video and camera during a meeting. This feature will make your meeting more professional. You can select the camera to be on or off. Click the drop up button for more features.

Screen Sharing in Instant Meeting

Click the share option from tool bar, choose screen to share. Choose entire screen, a specific application window or other content. The entire screen option is to share everything visible on your monitor, application window lets you share only a selected app. You can control the video how to appear on the screen with content only, as background, over the shoulder and side by side. Enable share sound to include computer audio, optimize for video sharing and share zoom window. Click “share” to start presenting your selected content.

End Meeting
